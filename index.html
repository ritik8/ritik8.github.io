  • How to play
  1. When you visit page you automatically have target.
  2. You can enter your name and click on submit.
  3. On left side your target and on right side your score.
  4. On right side their is buttons from 1 to 6 runs.
  5. You have click one at a time at same moment computer generate its output.
  6. If you and computer have same output then you out otherwise run is added to your score.
  7. Once the game is over means you win or lose click on new game.
  8. If you want to change name then reload the page.
